Product Case Study: Core Automation Flow

Zero-Touch Financial Onboarding

We have implemented an automation framework wherein the user only needs to enter the Material Receipt Note (MRN) or MRN Quality Check (MRN QC). All subsequent financial and accounting processes are handled completely automatically, eliminating human error.

This workflow triggers the auto-creation of the Purchase Invoice, unique Asset & Machine Code generation, ongoing Depreciation calculation, and immediate Journal Voucher (JV) posting mapped directly to the correct General Ledger (GL) accounts.

Advanced Depreciation

Ensures 100% statutory compliance without manual spreadsheet calculations.

  • Supports Straight Line Method (SLM) & Written Down Value (WDV) approaches.
  • Compliance-based reporting: Income Tax Act & Companies Act.
  • Automated Journal Voucher (JV) posting & General Ledger (GL) mapping.
  • Flexible calculation cycles: Monthly, Quarterly, Half-Yearly, Yearly.
📋

Acquisition & Master Data

Standardizes asset onboarding with rigorous technical precision.

  • Deep integration with Purchase Orders (PO), Gate Entry, and Material Receipt Notes (MRN).
  • Asset grouped by block-wise classification for Income Tax tracking.
  • Highly configurable specification templates to capture technical/operational attributes.
🔧

Proactive Maintenance

Maximizes equipment uptime and severely reduces breakdown overhead.

  • Tracks both Preventive and Breakdown maintenance schedules.
  • Automated Work Order generation and granular task tracking.
  • Calculates total maintenance cost per individual asset.
  • Auto-notifications dispatched instantly to maintenance teams.
📱

Mobile & QR Tracking

Eliminates ghost assets and accelerates physical audits by 80%.

  • Unique QR Codes map physical assets to exact digital specifications.
  • Real-time mobile app tracking for instant physical audits and verification.
  • Tracks employee-wise asset allocation and strict issue/return records.
  • Provides a complete, auditable asset movement log via barcode scanning.
🚚

Movement & Control

Secures corporate assets during transit and departmental shifts.

  • Gate pass workflow for Returnable (RGP) and Non-Returnable (NRGP) items.
  • Tracks inter-departmental and inter-location asset transfers securely.
  • Maintains extensive history tracking and documented asset custody records.
  • Direct integration with corporate Project & Capital Budget Management.
🛡️

Warranty & Insurance

Prevents revenue leakage by utilizing existing vendor coverages.

  • Centralizes policy details, insurer information, claims processing, and renewal tracking.
  • Automatic warranty coverage validation during Work Order creation to save costs.
  • Proactive alerts engine for upcoming Warranty & Insurance expiry dates.
